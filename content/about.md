#### About NIMGroup

The **Neural Intelligence in Medicine Group (NIMGroup)** is a research group dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence and machine learning in the fields of smart healthcare and intelligent medicine. We develop computational methods and AI-driven tools that bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and real-world clinical applications.

---

#### Research Focus

Our research spans three interconnected areas:

- **Neural Navigation** — developing intelligent navigation systems for neurosurgical procedures, leveraging imaging data and machine learning to enhance precision and safety in the operating room.

- **Image-Enabled Brain-Computer Interfaces** — combining medical imaging with BCI technology to create next-generation human-machine interfaces for diagnosis, rehabilitation, and cognitive augmentation.

- **AI for Smart Healthcare** — applying machine learning and deep learning to clinical decision support, medical image analysis, electronic health records, and healthcare data management.
